Subtractive Scheduling

This tool operates on the principle that in order for a group to find a good time to meet, it's helpful to start by identifying schedule conflicts. By surveying the conflicts, the overlapping viable times naturally emerge in the empty space.

Participants view the poll, select conflicting timeslots, and submit their responses. The best times to meet naturally emerge as the group responds to the poll.

This is free and open source software, and will never require an account or ask for your personal information.
