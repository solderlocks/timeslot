About Timeslot

This tool was inspired by the realization that finding a good meeting time is largely about working around schedule conflicts. By surveying the conflicts, the overlapping open times become visible in the empty space.

Participants view the poll, select timeslots that don't work for them, and submit their responses. The best times to meet naturally emerge as the group responds to the poll.

This is free software under the GNU GPL v3.0 license, and will never require an account or ask for your personal information.
