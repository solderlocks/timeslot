The Subtractive Method

Traditional scheduling tools ask everyone for their preferred times, creating a messy negotiation. Timeslot works backwards: assume everyone is available, and only mark the times you absolutely cannot make.

By finding the empty space, the group reaches consensus instantly without the back-and-forth of "what works best for you?"
